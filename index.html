ABOUT

My name is Melissa Portales and I'm a photographer currently based in Houston, Texas. My full-time career is in technology and I've applied my technical skills to my photography. My camera is my paint brush, it allows me to slow down and appreciate the world around me. So often we are going through life walking by the ordinary without taking the time to enjoy and explore. I hope my photography helps the viewer to recognize the beauty in our everyday environment, both in nature and in our manufactured surroundings.

Thanks for stopping by and taking a look.
