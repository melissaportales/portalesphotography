Fun Facts

Things I've learned about Bailey (my foster dog):

  1. He’s about a year old.
  2. He doesn’t snore.
  3. He loves to cuddle.
  4. He likes bath time and the blow dryer. He could do without the fur brushing but that part is a must.
  5. He is vocal about needing to go outside to potty, so far no accidents in the house.
  6. He might grow up to be a talker or maybe a singer.. he’s finding his voice, especially when I leave the house without him.
  7. He has the sit command down and I'm trying to teach him to give his paw (the hand shake, high five, fist bump, daps)... but he isn't interested just yet.
  8. He is good on a leash. However, like most puppies he gets overly excited when he see's a leash.. he's still learning to control that energy.
  9. He's played with kids ranging in ages from 1 1/2 years to 16 years old. They all love him. I'm pretty sure it's because he lets the kiddos pick the toys they want to play with first.
  10. He also plays well with other dogs. Even though he is neutered he WILL try to get frisky... just tell him to stop (maybe a couple of times) and he will.
  11. He’s definately ready for his forever home.
